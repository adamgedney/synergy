Est. 1997, Synergy Industries has been a staple in the northeast dance music scene. With almost 50 events ranging from a few hundred to thousands, its focus has always been on showcasing the freshest talent with a top notch production. Synergy Industries has teamed up with Snap Music it's next evolution of premiere music festivals.
